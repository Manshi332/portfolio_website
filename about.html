About Me

Hello, I'm Manshi Prajapati, a first-year student pursuing btech in Computer Science and Engineering at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna.

I completed my secondary education in 2020 from Datia, Madhya Pradesh. During my schooling, I dedicated myself to rigorous preparation for the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) along with maintaining academic excellence.

In 2023, my hard work and dedication paid off as I successfully cleared both JEE Mains and Advanced examinations, securing admission to IIT Patna in the first round of counseling.

I am currently focusing on web development and competitive programming, and I am deeply passionate about exploring new technologies. I enjoy the challenge of solving complex problems and am eager to learn and grow in these areas.I am excited to embark on this journey at IIT Patna to further improve my skills and contribute to the field.
